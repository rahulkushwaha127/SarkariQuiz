DISASTER MANAGEMENT TOPICS - INDIAN GOVERNMENT EXAMS (UPSC, SSC)

TYPES OF DISASTERS
- Natural Disasters (Earthquakes, Tsunamis, Floods, Droughts, Cyclones, Landslides, Avalanches)
- Geological Disasters (Earthquake, Volcanic Eruption, Landslide)
- Hydrological Disasters (Flood, Tsunami, Storm Surge)
- Meteorological Disasters (Cyclone, Hurricane, Typhoon, Tornado)
- Climatological Disasters (Drought, Wildfire, Heat Wave, Cold Wave)
- Biological Disasters (Epidemic, Pandemic, Pest Attack)
- Human-Induced Disasters (Nuclear Accident, Chemical Spill, Dam Collapse, Terrorist Attack, Cyber Attack)
- Industrial Accidents (Bhopal Gas Tragedy, etc.)
