HISTORY TOPICS FOR INDIAN GOVERNMENT EXAMS

ANCIENT INDIAN HISTORY - Prehistoric Age (Paleolithic, Mesolithic,
Neolithic) - Indus Valley Civilization - Vedic Period (Early & Later
Vedic) - Mahajanapadas - Buddhism and Jainism - Mauryan Empire -
Post-Mauryan Period (Shungas, Kushanas, Satavahanas) - Gupta Empire -
Regional Kingdoms

MEDIEVAL INDIAN HISTORY - Early Medieval Period - Rajput Kingdoms -
Chola, Pallava, Chera, Pandya Dynasties - Delhi Sultanate (Slave,
Khilji, Tughlaq, Sayyid, Lodi) - Mughal Empire - Vijayanagara Empire -
Bahmani & Deccan Sultanates - Bhakti and Sufi Movements

MODERN INDIAN HISTORY - Arrival of Europeans - British Expansion -
Revolt of 1857 - Social and Religious Reforms - Indian National Congress
Formation - Freedom Movement Phases - Gandhian Movements - Revolutionary
Movements - Towards Independence and Partition

INDIAN ART & CULTURE - Harappan Art - Buddhist Architecture - Temple
Architecture (Nagara, Dravida, Vesara) - Mughal Architecture -
Literature & Languages - Classical Dances & Music

WORLD HISTORY - Industrial Revolution - American Revolution - French
Revolution - World War I - World War II - Cold War
