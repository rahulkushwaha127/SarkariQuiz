TOPIC 1: Ancient Civilizations (Mesopotamia, Egypt, China, Persia)
================================================================================
Folder: Ancient_Civilizations

Subtopics:
- Mesopotamia: Sumer, Akkad, Babylon (Hammurabi), Assyria; cuneiform; Code of Hammurabi.
- Egypt: Old, Middle, New Kingdom; pyramids; pharaohs; hieroglyphics; Nile.
- China: Shang, Zhou, Qin (Shi Huangdi, Great Wall), Han; Confucianism, Taoism; Silk Road.
- Persia: Achaemenid Empire (Cyrus, Darius); Zoroastrianism; wars with Greece.

Files: 1–10 (causes, chronology, personalities, culture, impact).
